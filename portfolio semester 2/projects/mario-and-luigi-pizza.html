Mario and Luigi Pizza

This is a project I had in semester 1 in my studies before choosing a profile

In it, we made a website for a pizza shop for the legendary pizza makers, Mario and Luigi!

For it, we made sketches, paper and digital prototypes and documentation on what we are and not going to deliver

During the research and prototyping phase we discussed what we wanted to make and set limits for what our website would have and not have

The part I was responsible for was designing and making the order page for the website. While I was taking part in the rest of the process, this was my responsiblity to make

The main difficulty I had with it was the designing part, since I was busy with figuring out the coding part and removing any bugs I find

Since then, I've gotten better at coding since I'm getting more used to it again, but I still have trouble with the designing part and it takes me a while to figure out a nice looking design
